### Governance
#### Proposal 93
https://vote.uniswapfoundation.org/proposals/93
This proposal turns on fee burning - redirect profits from uniswap and unichain. Also burns 100m UNI from the treasury.

Implements a "tokenJar" and a "firepit" - fees are transferred to tokenjar, and fees can only be withdrawn from tokenjar if a corresponding burn happens in the firepit
